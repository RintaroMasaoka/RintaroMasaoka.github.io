LaTeX Unicode ⇌ Normal Converter

Customize Conversion Rules

You can edit the conversion rules in JSON format in the text area below.
After editing the rules, press the 'Save & Apply Rules' button.
They will be saved in local storage and available on your next visit.

correspondence: Basic Unicode and Normal mapping (bi-directional)
other_unicode: Alternative Unicode forms (used only for Unicode → Normal conversion)
other_normal: Alternative Normal forms (used only for Normal → Unicode conversion)

Each entry is in the format {"unicode": "...", "normal": "..."}.
